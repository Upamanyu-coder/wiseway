Documents Checklist for New Zealand

Your Gateway to New Zealand Starts with the Right Docs.

    For Offer Letter

    1. Application Form
    2. SEE Academics (Certificate, Marksheet, and Character Certificate)
    3. SLC Academics (Marksheet, Provisional, Migration, and Character Certificate)
    4. Bachelor's Degree Academics (Marksheet, Provisional, Migration, Character Certificate, and Degree Completion Certificate)
    5. English Proficiency Test Certificate
    6. Passport
    7. Letter of Recommendations (2 copies)
    8. Statement of Purpose (Signed by the Student)
    9. Updated CV
    10. Experience Certificate (If Any)

    For Visa

    1. Covering Letter (Word Format)
    2. Credit Card details to pay fees online (NZD 430 ‚âà NPR 21,500)
      • Card Holder Name
      • Expiry Date
      • CVV
      • Card Number
    3. Notary attested copy of First and Last pages of Passport
    4. Coloured scanned copy of all Passport pages (include old passports if available)
    5. Visa Forms:
      • INZ 1012 (click here to download forms)
      • INZ 1226 (click here to download forms)
      • Supplementary Information Form
    6. Visa Questionnaire Form
    7. Digital coloured photograph (45 mm x 35 mm, JPG, 381x528 pixels, 70%-80% face coverage, non-white light background)
    8. Offer Letter from Educational Institution
    9. Proof of Funds:
      • Bank Statement & Certificate / Education Loan Letter
      • Funds = 1st year Tuition + NZ$ 20,000 (Living) + NZ$ 2,000 (Airfare)
      • Funds should be 3‚Äì6 months old
      • Collateral Documents if loan is secured
      • Only one sponsor is accepted
    10. Email ID and Visiting Card of the bank branch for verification
    11. Financial Undertaking Form (INZ 1014) (click here to download forms)
    12. Affidavit of Support from sponsors (on NPR 100 Notary Stamp Paper)
    13. Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Passport copy of Sponsors
    14. Income Tax Returns (ITR) or Form 16 (Last 3 years)
      • Business Income: Provide GST registration etc.
      • If sponsors/parents are working abroad: Provide employment proof, salary account statement, and visa copy
    15. Student‚Äôs Birth Certificate (Optional)
    16. Notary attested Academic Documents (10th, 12th, Bachelor‚Äôs ‚Äî DigiLocker verified)
    17. Visa Refusal Letter (If any)
    18. Notary attested IELTS / PTE / TOEFL (PTE Login ID and Password if applicable)
    19. Work Experience Documents:
      • Experience Letter
      • Salary Account Statement
      • Form 16
      • ITR
    20. Medical Report (click here for more info) (from panel doctor, not older than 3 months)
    21. Police Clearance Certificate (not older than 6 months)
    22. Statement of Purpose (Word format)
    23. Updated Resume
    24. INZ 1025 (If sponsored by someone residing in New Zealand)
    25. Accepted Fund Types:
      • Secured Education Loan (collateral property from nationalized bank)
      • Savings Account (6-month statements)
      • Fixed Deposit
      • Provident Fund (with Withdrawable Limit Certificate)
      • LIC Policies (Surrender Value)
    26. Unaccepted Fund Types:
      • Cooperative Funds
      • Gold Loan / Sale of Gold
      • Unsecured Education Loan
      • Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Funds
      • Cash, Credit Card
      • Property Valuation or Documents
    27. Prepare using Mock Interview Questions (attached separately)

    Financial Requirements

    1. Notary Attested Affidavit of Support from Sponsor
    2. Max 2 Immediate Family Members as Sponsors
    3. INZ 1014 (Financial Undertaking Form) from each sponsor
    4. INZ 1025 (If sponsored by NZ resident)
    5. Annual Income Proof:
      • Salary Certificate
      • PAN Card
      • Business: Registration & PAN
      • Last 3 years Tax Clearance
      • Bank Statement
    6. Funds Required:
      • Course < 1 year: Tuition Fee + NZ$ 15,000 Living + NZ$ 2,000 Airfare
      • Course > 1 year: 1 year Tuition + NZ$ 15,000 + NZ$ 2,000 + Payment plan showing future fund availability
    7. Accepted Fund Types:
      • Secured Education Loan with Collateral (Nationalized Bank)
      • Savings (6 months statement)
      • Fixed Deposit
      • Provident Fund (Withdrawable Certificate)
      • LIC Policy (Surrender Value)
    8. Unaccepted Fund Types:
      • Co-operative funds
      • Gold Loan / Sale of Gold
      • Unsecured Education Loan
      • KCC Funds
      • Cash / Credit Cards
      • Property Documents or Valuation
