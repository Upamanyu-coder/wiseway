IELTS Preparation Classes

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is a globally recognized examination that assesses the English language proficiency of non-native speakers. It is widely accepted for purposes including higher education admissions, professional certification, and immigration. The assessment comprises four core modules: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

Test Duration: The total test time is approximately 2 hours and 45 minutes. The Listening, Reading, and Writing components are completed consecutively on the same day without breaks. The Speaking component may be scheduled either on the same day or within a few days of the other sections, depending on the test center's availability. Test Report Forms (TRFs) remain valid for two years.
