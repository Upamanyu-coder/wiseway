Pearson Test of English

The Pearson Test of English (PTE) is a computer-based English language proficiency assessment designed for non-native speakers. Recognized globally, it is widely accepted for academic admissions, immigration processes, and professional certification. The PTE evaluates candidates on four core skillsâ€”Speaking, Listening, Reading, and Writingâ€”through a range of integrated, real-life tasks such as reading aloud, summarizing spoken texts, and essay writing.

The test is available in two primary formats: PTE Academic, tailored for higher education and visa applications, and PTE General, which assesses general English proficiency. One of the key advantages of PTE is its efficiencyâ€”results are typically available within 48 hours. The fully automated scoring system ensures accuracy, consistency, and fairness. PTE scores range from 10 to 90, and the test is accepted by institutions and governments across the USA, UK, Australia, Canada, New Zealand, and beyond.
